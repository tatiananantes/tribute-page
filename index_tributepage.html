Sir Tim Berners-Lee

The man who invented the World Wide Web

Some of Sir Tim Berners-Lee biggest achievements:

  • Invented the World Wide Web (WWW) in 1989
  • Wrote the first web client and server in 1990
  • Cofounder and CTO of Inrupt.com
  • Director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
  • Director of the World Wide Web Foundation
  • Co-leads the Decentralized Information Group (DIG)
  • President and Founder of the Open Data Institute
  • Awarded the Turing Prize
